Nachos Recipe

Back to Index

Ingredients

  • 200g tortilla chips
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup black beans (optional)
  • 1/4 cup jalapeño slices
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup guacamole

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread the tortilla chips evenly on a baking tray or oven-safe dish.
  3. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the chips.
  4. Add diced tomatoes, black beans, jalapeños, and green onions on top.
  5. Bake in the oven for 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
  6. Remove from the oven and serve with sour cream and guacamole on the side.
